Understanding Knee Osteoarthritis

Knee osteoarthritis is a condition characterized by the degeneration of cartilage in the knee joint, causing pain, stiffness, and reduced mobility. Early diagnosis and management are crucial to improve quality of life and slow disease progression. This webpage provides tools to assist both medical professionals and individuals in assessing and managing knee osteoarthritis effectively.
