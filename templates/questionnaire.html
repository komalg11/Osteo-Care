Why Use This Questionnaire?

Our website aims to help users detect early signs of knee osteoarthritis using X-ray images. However, in cases where users do not have access to X-rays, this questionnaire provides a simple way to assess the likelihood of osteoarthritis based on your symptoms. If the questionnaire indicates a probability, we recommend consulting a doctor and using our model-based predictions for further insights.
